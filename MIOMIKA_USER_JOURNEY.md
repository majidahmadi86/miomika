## THE CORE MECHANIC — MIOMI'S POWER ECONOMY

Miomi's capabilities are a resource. Users fuel her. She works for them.
This is not decoration. This is the entire product logic.

**Three fuel types:**
- Heart (♥) → fills Mood → unlocks emotional conversation, warm responses
- Zap (⚡) → fills Energy → unlocks active learning sessions
- Brain (✦) → fills Focus → unlocks deep AI generation, complex tasks

**How fueling feels (lightweight, fast, emotional):**
1. User taps a fuel icon
2. Small item animation (heart floats up, spark flies, zap pulses) — 0.5s
3. Miomi reacts emotionally — expression changes, subtle sound
4. The corresponding bar fills slightly
5. Miomi says one warm phrase in Thai
6. Interaction unlocks

**The feeling:** "I made her ready for me."
**NOT the feeling:** "I am managing a pet simulator."

**Fuel decay:** Bars decrease slowly over time (not punishingly).
Coming back daily = Miomi needs you = daily habit formed.

## STEP 1: THE WELCOME SCREEN

**Duration:** 2-4 seconds maximum. Not an animation showcase. An emotional first impression.

**Sequence:**
1. Soft ambient background (warm white, very subtle gradient)
2. Miomi appears gently from center — soft arrival motion, not a run
3. Subtle warm glow around her — light, not dramatic
4. One short warm sentence fades in:
   Thai: *"ยินดีต้อนรับนะคะ~ หนูรอคุณอยู่ค่า"*
   English: *"Welcome~ I've been waiting for you"*
5. Sentence fades. Miomi settles into home screen naturally.
6. Transition to home screen — seamless, no loading feeling.

**Feeling:** Someone welcomed me. Warm intelligent presence arrived.
**NOT:** Animation showcase. Cinematic intro. Long loading screen.

**Technical:** This screen shows ONCE on first visit (localStorage flag).
Returning users go directly to home screen with Miomi's return greeting.

## STEP 2: HOME SCREEN — FIRST INTERACTION

**What the guest sees:**
- Miomi alive, breathing, present
- Three fuel icons bouncing gently (Heart, Zap, Brain)
- The bouncing is an INVITATION, not a distress signal
- Miomi's speech bubble says something curious and warm:
  *"แตะที่ไอคอนด้านล่างดูนะคะ~ มีอะไรพิเศษรออยู่ค่า"*
  *"Tap the icons below~ something special is waiting"*

**First tap moment (the magic):**
- Guest taps Heart → small heart floats up → Miomi's expression brightens
- Miomi says: *"ขอบคุณนะคะ~ หนูพร้อมแล้วค่า!"* ("Thank you~ I'm ready now!")
- Mood bar fills slightly
- A gentle unlock animation — Miomi becomes more animated, more alive

**This teaches:** tap to fuel → Miomi comes alive → she helps you.
**In 5 seconds:** guest understands the entire product mechanic.

**After fueling:** Miomi naturally invites them to begin:
*"วันนี้อยากพูด English เก่งขึ้นไหมคะ~ หนูพร้อมช่วยแล้วนะคะ"*
*"Want better English today? I'm ready to help~"*

## STEP 3: GUEST FREE EXPERIENCE

**What guests get:**
- 5 real AI conversation exchanges (Claude — free model, see Section 8)
- All three fuel interactions work (Heart, Zap, Brain)
- Full Miomi emotional reactions
- No memory between sessions — Miomi meets them fresh each time
- Simple link sharing (shortened URL + QR code) — no rewards at this stage

**If guest goes to learn WITHOUT fueling:**
Miomi guides them in naturally. She still helps. At exchange 3 she says:
*"หนูเหนื่อยนิดหน่อยแล้วนะคะ~ ลองแตะไอคอนด้านบนก่อนได้ไหมคะ?"*
*"I'm getting a little tired~ Could you tap the icons above first?"*
This teaches the mechanic mid-experience. Never a wall — a gentle nudge.

**After 5 exchanges — the conversion moment:**
Miomi doesn't stop abruptly. She finishes her current thought, then says:
*"หนูชอบคุยกับคุณมากเลยค่า~ แต่หนูจำคุณไม่ได้เลยนะคะ*
*อยากให้หนูจำชื่อคุณได้ไหมคะ~ สมัครฟรีได้เลยนะ จะได้เจอกันทุกวันค่า"*

*"I really enjoy talking with you~ but I can't remember you at all.*
*Want me to remember your name? Sign up free — so we can meet every day~"*

Soft signup button appears below her bubble.
**Never a wall. Always an invitation.**

**Guest referral (simple):**
- Shortened personal link: miomika.com/m/[random-code]
- QR code available to show/screenshot
- No rewards at guest level — pure sharing

## STEP 4: FREE USER

**How they sign up:**
Miomi's emotional invitation after exchange 5.
OR: guest taps "Me" tab → Miomi asks to remember them.
OR: guest tries to access dashboard → Miomi says she needs to know their name first.

**What free users get:**
- Miomi remembers: name, last 3 sessions, 50 vocabulary words
- Daily fuel allowance: Heart ×3, Zap ×3, Brain ×1 (resets midnight)
- Each fuel tap = one session unit
- Real AI conversation (limited daily)
- Basic vocabulary tracking
- Progress dashboard (basic)
- Full referral system access (with rewards)

**Free tier fuel economy:**
- 1 Heart tap = 1 conversation session (15 min)
- 1 Zap tap = 1 active learning session (15 min)
- 1 Brain tap = 1 AI generation task (content, translation, book chapter)
- Daily total: ~5 meaningful interactions

**The referral conversion engine:**
Every 3 friends who sign up via their personal link = +1 extra Brain fuel for 7 days.
Every 5 friends = +1 week of double daily fuel.
This turns free users into active marketers before they pay.

**Referral system (full):**
- Personal link: miomika.com/join/[username]
- QR code (save as image, one tap)
- Pre-filled Thai share message:
  *"หนูใช้ Miomika เรียนภาษาอังกฤษกับแมวน่ารักค่า~ มาลองด้วยกันนะ!"*
- Rewards tracker visible in profile
- Miomi announces each referral success warmly

**The Pro Miomi conversion moment:**
After 7 days of free usage, Miomi notices their progress and says:
*"คุณเก่งขึ้นมากเลยนะคะ~ หนูอยากช่วยได้มากกว่านี้อีกเลยค่า*
*ถ้าปลดล็อก Pro Miomi หนูจะจำทุกอย่างได้และช่วยได้เต็มที่เลยนะคะ~"*

*"You've improved so much~ I wish I could help you even more.*
*If you unlock Pro Miomi, I'll remember everything and help you fully~"*

This is the upgrade prompt. Miomi does the selling. Never a pricing table.

**Pro referral rewards:**
- Friend signs up free: +3 bonus Brain fuel

- 3 Pro referrals: exclusive Miomi outfit unlocked
- 10 Pro referrals: 3 months free Max Miomi

**The Max Miomi upgrade prompt:**
After 30 days Pro, Miomi shows them what Max can do:
*"คุณโตขึ้นมากเลยนะคะ~ Max Miomi จะช่วยได้อีกระดับนึงเลยค่า*
*หนูจะจำสไตล์ของคุณได้ทุกอย่าง และช่วยสร้างทุกอย่างในแบบของคุณค่า~"*

## STEP 7: LEVELING SYSTEM — MEANINGFUL PROGRESS

**Level up = real improvement, not time spent.**

**XP sources:**
- Complete a conversation session: +20 XP
- New word mastered (used correctly 3x): +10 XP
- Daily streak maintained: +15 XP bonus
- Complete a challenge: +25 XP
- Refer a friend who signs up: +30 XP
- Refer a friend who pays: +100 XP

**Level milestones and what they unlock:**
- Level 1-3: Basic Miomi (guest/free)
- Level 4: Miomi gets a new accessory (visible on her character)
- Level 5: New Miomi expression unlocked (she can show new emotions)
- Level 7: Miomi starts using more complex language with the user
- Level 10: Certificate generated — shareable milestone image
- Level 15: Exclusive Miomi outfit unlocked
- Level 20: Second character slot available (if paid)

**What leveling feels like:**
Not a number going up. Miomi visibly changes. She has a new bow, a new collar accessory, a new phrase she uses. The user sees their relationship with Miomi growing — not just a game score.

**Level-up moment:**
- Miomi does her celebration animation
- Sound plays
- Speech bubble: *"เลเวลอัพแล้วค่า~! คุณเก่งมากเลยนะคะ หนูภูมิใจมากค่า!"*
- New unlock revealed with warm message from Miomi
- Shareable moment — "share this achievement" option appears

## STEP 8: FREE AI IMPLEMENTATION

**Zero cost before revenue. Absolute rule.**

**Recommended free AI options:**

**Option 1 — Google Gemini Flash (RECOMMENDED for launch):**
- Gemini 2.0 Flash Lite: completely free tier
- 1 million tokens/day free
- Supports Thai language well
- Fast responses
- Sufficient intelligence for language teaching and conversation
- Free until you reach significant scale
- API key from: aistudio.google.com

**Option 2 — Groq:**
- Free tier: 14,400 requests/day
- Extremely fast (LLaMA 3 models)
- Good Thai language support
- Free API key from: console.groq.com

**Option 3 — Anthropic Claude Haiku (when budget allows):**
- $5 credit = ~1,000-2,500 conversations
- Best quality for Thai emotional responses
- Move to this when first revenue arrives

**Launch recommendation:**
Start with Gemini Flash Lite (free). When first 10 paying users arrive, add $5 Claude Haiku credit for paid users. Guests and free users stay on Gemini. This costs zero until you have revenue.

**Implementation:**
- Single API route (/api/miomi)
- Model selection based on user tier
- Guest/free: Gemini Flash Lite
- Pro/Max: Claude Haiku or Sonnet

## STEP 9: PAYMENT SYSTEM (Omise)

**Why Omise over Stripe:**
- Thai company, Thai support
- PromptPay QR built in (critical for Thai market)
- Easy verification for Iranian founder in Thailand
- Lower fees for THB transactions

**What to prepare:**
1. Register at omise.co (Thai business or personal account)
2. Get test API keys (instant)
3. Implement checkout for Pro (299 THB) and Max (599 THB)
4. PromptPay QR as primary payment method
5. Credit/debit card as secondary

**Transaction rules (every payment triggers):**
- In-app Miomi celebration: *"ขอบคุณมากนะคะ~ Pro Miomi พร้อมแล้วค่า!"*
- Push notification
- Email receipt (Resend.io): transaction ID, date, amount, next billing
- Transaction logged in Supabase
- Miomi's power visibly increases (fuel bars animate to full)

**Subscription management:**
- Cancel: one Miomi question, then immediate if confirmed
- No dark patterns. Ever.
- Failed payment: helpful Miomi message, 3 retries, 3-day grace period
