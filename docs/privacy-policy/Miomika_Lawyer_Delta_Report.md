# Miomika — Lawyer Delta Report

**Prepared: 15 June 2026 · For a future licensed Thai lawyer**

This report exists to **minimise your legal bill**. The Terms of Service and Privacy Policy have already been drafted conservatively by the operator and are running in beta. You do **not** need to re-draft them. This report lists only the items that genuinely require a licensed Thai lawyer's judgement. Everything not listed here has been handled conservatively by the operator and is documented in the accompanying Risk Register.

**Context for you:** Miomika is a voice-first, 18+ AI language-learning app (companion character "Miomi"), operated by Mikaro Studio, Bangkok. Beta restricts users to adults, does not train AI on user data, does not sell data, and treats conversation content as sensitive with explicit consent. Payments via Stripe and Omise; some processors are outside Thailand.

---

## What we need from you (scope-limited)

### D1 — Liability and disclaimer enforceability *(priority: high)*
Review the limitation-of-liability, disclaimer, and "as is" clauses (ToS §11–12) and tell us how far they are enforceable under Thai consumer-protection law, and how to word them so they hold up. Confirm the mandatory-rights carve-out is correct.

### D2 — Sensitive-data consent wording *(priority: high)*
Confirm whether user conversation content should be treated as "sensitive personal data" under PDPA s.26, and approve (or correct) the explicit-consent wording we use at onboarding and in Privacy §3–4.

### D3 — Cross-border transfer basis *(priority: medium-high)*
We use processors outside Thailand (AI infrastructure, hosting). Confirm the correct lawful transfer mechanism under PDPA for our destinations, and what we must add to Privacy §7. We will provide our actual vendor/country list.

### D4 — Age gate and minor-safety posture *(priority: medium)*
We restrict beta to 18+. Confirm this is sufficient as drafted, what age-affirmation we must implement, and what would change legally if we later open the Service to under-18 users (which the companion category makes likely to come up).

### D5 — Subscription, auto-renewal, and refund compliance *(priority: medium)*
Confirm our auto-renewal disclosure and 14-day beta refund approach (ToS §5) meet Thai consumer-protection requirements, including any mandatory pre-purchase disclosures.

### D6 — DPO and PDPA registration obligations *(priority: medium)*
Advise whether Mikaro Studio must appoint a Data Protection Officer and/or complete any PDPA registration given our processing activities and scale, and confirm our breach-notification obligations and timelines.

- Translate help-center, onboarding, or marketing copy (non-legal; the operator handles those).
- Review IP/user-content licence terms unless time permits (low risk; no AI training, no content resale).
